The Problem

Code runs on living systems: databases with millions of rows, APIs under real load, infrastructure tuned for today's customers.

Code Review Only Sees Code. Traditional reviewers stop at syntax and patterns. They deliver the same advice whether you're serving 100 or 100,000 users, so scale-specific failures slip through.

Production Incidents Come From Context. Outages happen because loops fan out across millions of rows, missing indexes stretch queries into seconds, and schema tweaks ripple through forgotten code paths. These issues are only visible when you understand the system, not just the code.

What is Ravi

Ravi is your team's resident architect, bringing system-level awareness to every pull request. Software engineering isn't about writing textbook-perfect code. It's about making the right trade-offs for your constraints. Ravi helps you make those choices without compromising velocity.

System-Aware Reviews

With visibility into your database structure, query execution plans, production workloads, and live traffic patterns, Ravi catches what code-only reviewers miss. That N+1 query might handle 10 req/min fine, but at 500 req/min with 5 million rows? It's an outage waiting to happen.

True Peer, Not a Linter

Other code reviewers give the same advice to a 1,000-user startup as they would to Google. Ravi understands your current reality: where you are today, not where you might be in five years. Early stage? Ship fast. At scale? Prioritize stability. Just like a senior engineer would.

Built for Engineering Leaders

You can't hire a senior architect for every team, but you can scale design-level expertise across your organization. Ravi ensures every PR gets thoughtful, context-aware review: catching production issues before they ship, without slowing anyone down.
