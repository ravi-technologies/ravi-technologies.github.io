How Ravi Works

High-Signal, Production-Focused Reviews

Ravi only flags issues that will materially impact production, keeping your team moving fast without sacrificing reliability.

  • Production-Impact Focus: Only surface issues that matter for your actual workload, skipping the noise of generic linters and style nits
  • Data-Driven Analysis: Catch N+1 queries, missing indexes, and scaling bottlenecks by analyzing actual table sizes, query costs, and execution patterns
  • Preserve Developer Velocity: Every suggestion is actionable and grounded in production reality, so teams can ship confidently without drowning in false positives
